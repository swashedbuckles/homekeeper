Kitchen Renovation Planning Guide

This document serves as a comprehensive guide for planning and executing a kitchen renovation project. It covers everything from initial planning to final inspections.

Pre-Planning Phase

Budget Considerations

Before starting any renovation work, establish a clear budget that includes:

  • Appliances - 35-40% of total budget
  • Labor - 20-25% of total budget
  • Cabinets & Hardware - 35-40% of total budget
  • Contingency Fund - 10-20% for unexpected costs

Design Planning

Consider the kitchen work triangle and traffic flow when planning your layout. The three main work areas are:

  1. Refrigerator/Storage Area
  2. Sink/Cleanup Area
  3. Stove/Cooking Area
Pro Tip: The total distance of the work triangle should be between 12-26 feet for optimal efficiency.

Installation Timeline

Week 1-2: Demolition & Preparation

  • Remove old appliances and cabinetry
  • Update electrical and plumbing as needed
  • Install new flooring

Week 3-4: Installation

  • Install new cabinets
  • Install countertops
  • Install backsplash
  • Install appliances

Maintenance Schedule

Once renovation is complete, establish a regular maintenance schedule:

Monthly Tasks:
- Clean range hood filter
- Check garbage disposal operation
- Inspect cabinet hardware

Quarterly Tasks:
- Deep clean appliances
- Check caulking around sink
- Inspect under-cabinet lighting

Annual Tasks:
- Service range hood
- Check appliance warranties
- Update equipment manuals
